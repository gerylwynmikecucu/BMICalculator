Tips on how to fight Underweight

  • Consume more calories than you burn. Choose healthy foods to add calories.
  • Eat healthy food frequently, 5 to 6 times a day.
  • Include protein food in your diet because protein is the key for the muscle mass.
  • Avoid fatty foods and go with healthy fats.
  • Reduce as much mental and emotional stress as possible.
  • Take rest at least for 8 hours.
  • Exercise regularly.
